---
title: "State Replaces Permanent Fund Dividend Checks With Single Vending Machine in Juneau"
date: 2025-11-02
excerpt: "Every Alaskan is technically eligible. Getting there is, per state officials, 'your problem now.'"
author: Capitol Bureau
---

In a cost-saving move announced Monday, the State of Alaska will replace this year's Permanent Fund Dividend checks with a single vending machine installed in a Juneau parking structure.

"Every resident is still entitled to their full dividend," said a spokesperson for the Department of Revenue. "They just need to physically insert their ID card into the machine, in person, before March."

The machine, which dispenses dividends in the form of gold-wrapped chocolate coins and a printed receipt redeemable "at a later date," has already generated a line reportedly visible from the state ferry terminal.

Residents of Utqiaġvik, roughly 800 miles away, have been advised to "leave early."
