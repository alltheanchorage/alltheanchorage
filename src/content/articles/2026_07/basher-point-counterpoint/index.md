**POINT: The Basher Trailhead parking lot is perfect just the way it is**

Do we really need an expanded parking lot at the Basher Trailhead? I have lived in the area for over 30 years, and I have never seen that parking lot reach capacity. It’s perfect just the way it is! Yes, there may be some minor potholes, and yes I suppose that SOMETIMES it fills up, but for the most part it is good enough. You know how when people move into a bigger house they end up filling it up with junk, even if before moving they didn’t have that much stuff? I think that is what is going to happen to the Basher Trailhead, in that the extra spaces will encourage more and more people to use the trailhead to access Chugach. And what a shame that would be.
