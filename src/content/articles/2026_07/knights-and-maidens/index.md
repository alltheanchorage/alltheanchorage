Knights and maidens have been spotted wandering around Russian Jack Springs Park weeks after the conclusion of the renaissance fair. “What be your mission, lad?” asked one of the knights wandering around Russian Jack during an interview with a reporter. “My King hath sent me. Evil beasts lurk these dark woods, friend, and ‘tis my duty to defeat them. From the misty shores of Pine Street to the towering mountains of Boniface, this great kingdom of Russian Jack must be protected!”

“Excuse me, my lord, could you lend a hand with this water?” called out one of the maidens, gesturing to wooden buckets of murky water on the ground. “Tis for supper, if you’ve any interest in joining? The road is long, aye, and you look weary, so a warm meal may do some good,” continued the maiden as she kept wandering around Russian Jack. “Anyways, there are mischievous creatures lurking about, some with great big antlers and long legs the likes of which I have never seen, so it's best that you don't linger long.” At press time, superheroes and cartoon characters were seen wandering around the Dena’ina Center months after the conclusion of comic con.
